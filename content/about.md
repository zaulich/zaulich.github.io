## Me

I'm a Microsoft 365 architect working with large-scale migrations, automation, and the occasional "why is Teams doing this?" moment. 🧑‍💻

Most of my day is spent moving tenants, untangling complex environments, and writing PowerShell scripts that automate things Microsoft probably intended to be clicked in the portal.

I enjoy turning messy cloud environments into something structured and predictable — preferably with a good script, a clear diagram, and minimal user impact. ⚡

This blog is mostly a collection of practical Microsoft 365 tips, PowerShell snippets, and discoveries that might save someone else a few hours of troubleshooting. 📦
